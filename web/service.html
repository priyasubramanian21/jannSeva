Weight Loss

Obesity or over weight is a metabolic disorder involving an excessive amount of body fat. It's a medical problem that increases the risk of other diseases and health problems, such as heart disease, diabetes, high blood pressure and certain cancers. It may be because of genetics or modern life style

Our weight loss programme is an unique program which contains different types of aerobic and anaerobic patterns of physical exercise that combines rhythmic exercise with stretching, strength and endurance training routines with the goal of improving all elements of motor abilities. Its benefit ranges to different areas depends upon its aim. Cardiovascular endurance, muscular endurance, neuro muscular coordination, immunity development, are the major benefits. As it is an endurance workout the body use fat energy which is stored in our body.
