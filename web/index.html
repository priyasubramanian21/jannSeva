Why You Should Go To Yoga

Yoga is a popular exercise that focuses on breathing, strength, and flexibility. Practicing yoga may provide many physical and mental health benefits. The overall philosophy of yoga is about connecting the mind, body, and spirit.

Eight limbs of yoga

Patanjali set out Yoga Sutras as having eight limbs as follows:

  • yama (abstinences),
  • niyama (observances),
  • asana (yoga postures),
  • pranayama (breath control),
  • pratyahara (withdrawal of the senses),
  • pratyahara (withdrawal of the senses),
  • dhyana (meditation)
  • samadhi (absorption)
